A Design of Piggery Management System for Heat Stress


The project is a microcontroller-controlled system that can detect, prevent, and treat pigs suffering from heat stress. The thermal camera will detect heat emanating from a pig's body and transmit it to the microcontroller as an input. Additionally, it is stored in the system's database for the purpose of logging data that is useful to the Piggery Management System.
